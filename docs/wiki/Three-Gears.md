# Three Gears

Darce has three modes that control how much the AI explains while it works. Switch anytime with `Ctrl+1/2/3` or from the View menu.

## Ship (Ctrl+1)

Maximum speed. The AI builds and moves on. No explanations, no interruptions. Use when you know what you want and just need it done fast.

The teaching panel is hidden in Ship mode.

## Understand (Ctrl+2)

The AI builds AND explains. After each action, it breaks down the patterns, decisions, and alternatives. You see:

- **Concepts** — What patterns were used (React hooks, CSS grid, etc.)
- **Explanations** — Why this approach, what alternatives exist
- **Tags** — Categorized by technology (react, css, js, node, etc.)
- **Difficulty** — Beginner, intermediate, or advanced

Switch between depth levels:
- **Brief** — 2-3 concepts, one-line explanations
- **Standard** — 3-5 concepts with practical detail
- **Deep** — 4-6 concepts with code examples and gotchas
- **ELI5** — Explain Like I'm 5 — simple analogies, no jargon

## Learn (Ctrl+3)

Everything from Understand, plus:

- **Before coding** — The AI asks what approach YOU would take
- **After coding** — Interactive quiz to test your understanding
- **Spaced repetition** — Concepts reinforce over time

Learn mode turns every coding session into a learning session. You don't just get working code — you understand why it works.
